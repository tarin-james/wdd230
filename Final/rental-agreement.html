Scoots Rental Agreement and Information

    Agreement Overview:

    This rental agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the renter ("Renter") and Scoots, a motor scooter rental company located in Cozumel, Mexico. By renting a motor scooter from Scoots, the Renter agrees to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement.

  1. Rental Period:

    The rental period begins at the time of scooter pickup and ends at the time of scooter return, as specified in the rental agreement.

  2. Rental Requirments:
    • Renter must be at least 18 years of age and possess a valid driver's license.
    • Renter must provide a valid credit card for security deposit purposes.
    • Renter is responsible for any damages or losses incurred during the rental period.
  3. Rental Rates:
    • Rental rates vary based on the type of scooter and rental duration. Please refer to the rental pricing chart for specific rates.
  4. Saftey Precautions:
    • Renter must wear a helmet and adhere to all traffic laws and regulations while operating the scooter.
    • Scoots recommends wearing appropriate protective gear, including closed-toe shoes and eye protection.
    • Renter should inspect the scooter before operation and report any damages or issues to Scoots immediately.
  5. Liability Waiver:
    • Renter acknowledges that operating a motor scooter carries inherent risks and releases Scoots from any liability for personal injury or property damage resulting from scooter rental.
  6. Insurance Coverage:
    • Scoots provides basic insurance coverage for the rented scooter. Additional insurance options are available for purchase.
  7. Reservation and Cancellation Policy:
    • Reservations are recommended to guarantee scooter availability. A non-refundable deposit may be required for reservations.
    • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fees.
  8. Additional Services:
    • Scoots offers optional services, such as scooter delivery and guided tours, for an additional fee.
  9. Rental Return
    • Renter must return the scooter to the designated rental location at the agreed-upon time. Late returns may incur additional charges.
  10. Governing Law
    • This Agreement is governed by the laws of Mexico. Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Cozumel.

By filling out the reservation form, the Renter acknowledges that they have read and understood the terms and conditions of this Agreement.
