Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership (Non-Profit):

  • No membership fee required
  • Opportunities to network and collaborate with other non-profit entities.
  • Featured promotion of your organization's events on our platform.
  • Discounted rates for chamber-hosted events and programs.

Bronze Membership

  • Invitation to exclusive networking events and seminars.
  • Discounted rates for chamber-sponsored workshops and training sessions.
  • Opportunity for occasional spotlight features on our homepage.

Silver Membership:

  • Priority listing and enhanced visibility on our website directory.
  • Access to premium networking events with industry leaders and influencers.
  • Exclusive discounts on advertising packages for increased exposure.
  • Complimentary admission to select chamber-sponsored events and workshops.

Gold Membership

  • Prominent placement and priority listing on our website directory, ensuring maximum visibility.
  • VIP access to exclusive networking receptions and high-profile events.
  • Premium advertising opportunities, including featured spotlights on our homepage.
  • Complimentary admission to all chamber-sponsored events, workshops, and seminars.
  • Personalized support from chamber staff to address your specific business needs and goals.
