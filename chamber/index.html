Monthly Spotlight

Nestled in the heart of Rexburg, Alpine Jewelers stands as a beacon of elegance and craftsmanship in the local community. This business has been a cherished part of Rexburg's landscape for over two decades, specializing in creating timeless and personalized jewelry pieces. Alpine Jewelers goes beyond being a business; it's a vital part of the Rexburg community. Actively involved in local events and charities, Alpine strives to give back to the community that has supported them over the years.
